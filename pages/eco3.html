EcoBite

Think before you Bite! Reshaping the culture of eating out through data-driven sustainability.

Project Demonstration

Role

Creative Coder & Full-Stack Developer

Core Tech

React, Mapbox GL, FastAPI, OpenAI

Target Audience

NYU Students & Conscious Foodies

● The Problem

Eating out is at the heart of New York culture, but the hidden environmental and carbon impacts of every meal choices add up quickly. Students want to make greener decisions, but reliable restaurant eco-data is completely opaque and inaccessible.

● The Solution

A vibrant social platform to promote sustainable eating habits. EcoBite maps and ranks localized neighborhoods across Manhattan and Brooklyn, enabling users to view eco-scores, scan food menus with AI, and track collective carbon savings through an engaging, gamified plant avatar.

Interface Architecture

Seamless mapping interfaces paired with sidebar insights built to display restaurant scores at a single glance.

The Green Score Model

Calculated transparently using real-time NYC Open Data parameters via the following allocation weights:

50 pts
Energy Efficiency

Sourced from NYC LL84 Building Data scaled directly using building Energy Star Scores.

25 pts
Water Efficiency

Evaluates Water Use Intensity metrics against local New York city-wide medians.

25 pts
Health Grade

Derived from ongoing DOHMH inspection scores and sanitation letter records.

🟢 Tier Green: ≥70 🟡 Tier Yellow: 50-69 🔴 Tier Red: <50

Interactive Features

1

Neighborhood Map Navigation

Interactive Mapbox GL polygons built over 10 custom Lower Manhattan & Brooklyn zones with responsive fly-to search mechanics.

2

AI Menu Scanner

Powered by OpenAI GPT-4o-mini Vision models to read uploaded snapshot elements and accurately break down target ingredient footprints.

3

Gamified Plant Avatar

Encourages ongoing user commitment through a visual seed-to-bloom asset tracker that responds to cumulative logged CO₂ scores.

4

Granular Filters

Enables immediate toggling between full restaurant locations or specialized 'Sips & Sweets' cafes and snack spaces.
