Interactive Features

Each piece is built as a standalone p5.js sketch wrapped in a React component, so the narrative logic and the interaction logic can evolve independently.

Ondel-Ondel Experience
Nine-scene narrative progression telling the guardian spirits' story
Background elements that respond in real time to mouse movement
Color shifts that represent emotional states across the story
Barong Experience
Kaleidoscope drawing mode with mirrored, symmetrical patterns
20+ image layers composited into fluid animation
Background music via p5.sound for an immersive mood
A sticker generator so visitors can collect their own Barong

Design Philosophy

Merangkai — Indonesian for "to weave together" — sits at the intersection of cultural heritage and modern web technology. The goal isn't to digitize tradition for its own sake, but to translate it faithfully: Bebas Neue carries the boldness of the cultural statements being made, IBM Plex Mono keeps the interface clean and modern, and every interaction is built to invite people into a culture rather than flatten it into decoration.

Challenges Overcome

Translating folklore and mythology into interaction design without losing cultural nuance
Managing 20+ layered animations and audio inside a React component lifecycle
Building a generative batik system that respects real pattern logic, not just noise
